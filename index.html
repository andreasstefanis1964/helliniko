About Hellinikon Hotel & Studios

Just 90 m from Livadia Beach in Parikia & 600 meters from the port of Parikia in Paros.

Built in traditional Cycladic style full of bougainvilleas you will enjoy your staying with local hospitality in a cosy family hotel. Set 500 metres from Livadia, Helliniko Hotel & Studios offers a garden, a terrace and air-conditioned accommodation with a balcony and free WiFi. The units come with tiled floors and feature a fully equipped kitchenette with a fridge, a flat-screen TV, and a private bathroom with shower and a hair dryer. A patio with outdoor furniture and garden views is offered in every unit. At the apartment complex, the units include bed linen and towels.

Helliniko Hotel & Studios located 900 meters from the port of Parikia. Just 90 meters from Helliniko Studio you will find the first beach of parikia "Livadia". Near Helliniko Hotel & Studios you will find cafeterias, bakery, mini market and several and different type of tavernas and restaurants. Helliniko Studio is surrounding from a beautiful garden with trees and many fruits that you may enjoy at any time.

Discover Paros island

Paros is a captivating Greek island in the heart of the Cyclades, offering a wonderful blend of natural beauty, historical charm, and vibrant culture.

Paros boasts some of the most stunning beaches in the Aegean. From the popular Golden Beach with its golden sands and water sports to the more secluded and picturesque Kolymbithres, there's a beach for every preference. The island is dotted with traditional Cycladic villages characterized by narrow alleys, white-washed houses adorned with bougainvillea, and vibrant blue-domed churches. Naoussa and Parikia are two particularly charming towns to explore. Naoussa, a picturesque fishing village in the northern part of the island, is known for its charming harbor, traditional Greek architecture, and vibrant nightlife. The island is renowned for its excellent wind conditions, making it a popular destination for windsurfers and kitesurfers. Pounda Beach, in particular, is famous for its water sports facilities.
